Welcome to MedaMax

We are a passionate team from Bengaluru striving to make PHC accessible in the cities and towns around Bengaluru. We have opened our first experience store in Sakkarenadu Mandya.

How it works:

  1. You enter our pharmacy in Ashok Nagar
  2. You ask the pharmacist (health expert) for the free doctor consultation
  3. You provide basic details such as your name, age, and condition
  4. The pharmacist (health expert) will contact the doctor and take you to the consultation room
  5. Basic checks such as weight, temperature, blood pressure, and blood sugar (as mentioned by the doctor) are done
  6. You meet the doctor face to face via online mode through our tablet
  7. You consult the doctor like you would in any clinic and exit the call
  8. The doctor sends the prescription to the pharmacist
  9. The pharmacist provides you with the medication and a follow-up date (if required)
  10. You pay only for the medicines, collect them and leave

What is in it for us:

We strive for a Karnataka where basic amenities such as healthcare are accessible to all. We have started with the vision to make our state healthier and stronger. Your victory here is our victory.
